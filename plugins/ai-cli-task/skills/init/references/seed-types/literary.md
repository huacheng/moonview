# literary

## Description

Fiction, poetry, creative writing, essays

## Methodology

Style analysis, consistency checks, readability

## Phase Intelligence

### plan

- **Collection Direction**: Genre conventions, narrative structure theory, character development frameworks
- **Key Sources**: Writer's Digest, Save the Cat, Story Grid, genre-specific style guides, literary criticism
- **Plan Structure**: Theme/premise → structure/outline → character design → scene drafting → revision
- **Key Considerations**: Narrative arc, voice consistency, character development, pacing, stylistic coherence

### verify

- **Collection Direction**: Editorial checklists, consistency checking, readability scoring
- **Key Sources**: ProWritingAid, Grammarly API, Flesch-Kincaid, custom continuity checkers
- **Quick Checkpoint**: Word count within target range, no encoding issues
- **Full Checkpoint**: Narrative arc completeness, character consistency across chapters, style guide adherence, pacing analysis
- **Key Tools**: Manual checklist + automated: `proselint`, `vale` (custom style), `wc`, diff against outline

### check

- **Collection Direction**: Editorial standards, genre expectations, publication requirements
- **Key Sources**: Publishing house style guides, genre conventions, beta reader feedback criteria
- **Indicators**: Fiction, poetry, creative writing, essay
- **Verification Approach**: Structural completeness, narrative consistency, style guide adherence, character/plot continuity

### exec

- **Collection Direction**: Writing craft techniques, genre-specific conventions, revision strategies
- **Key Sources**: Craft books, genre writing guides, editing technique references
- **Implementation Approach**: Draft/revise text, maintain character/plot notes, apply style guidelines
- **Step Verification**: Narrative consistency check, structural completeness, style coherence review
